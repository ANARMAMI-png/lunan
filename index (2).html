Android 8.0 and above · APK via Firebase

How to install

1
Tap "Download" above

You'll be taken to the Firebase download page. Sign in with any Google account when prompted.

2
Allow unknown sources

Android may block the install. If it does, tap the prompt or go to:

Settings → Security (or Privacy)
→ Install unknown apps → Allow your browser
3
Open the downloaded APK

Go to your Downloads folder or notification bar, tap the lunan.apk file and hit Install.

4
Open Lunan and explore!

Create an account or log in. Browse venues, book a spot, and enjoy.

Why isn't this on the Play Store?
Lunan is a student capstone project showcased at our school's Entrepreneur's Mind Expo. The app is safe — it's distributed via Google's own Firebase platform.
